# Kaja Zajda

**Senior Business Analyst · GenAI Specialist · Neurodiversity Advocate**

I work at the intersection of AI systems, human cognition, and applied ethics. My day job is building AI adoption frameworks for the insurance industry. My other work is writing about neurodiversity and productivity in the age of AI.

---

### Current projects

**[ScamRadar PL](https://github.com/zajdakaja/scamradar-pl)**
Browser extension (Chrome + Firefox Android) that detects deepfake scam ads impersonating Polish banks in real time — no ML, no backend, no data collection. Uses a six-signal proxy heuristic engine to fingerprint scam context instead of analyzing video frames.

**Właściwy Klucz** *(in progress)*
A Polish-language book on neurodiversity and AI productivity. Exploring how ADHD, autism, and dyslexia interact with AI tools — and why "productivity" as a concept needs redesigning for non-standard cognition.
